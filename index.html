About Me

My research practice involves working at the intersections of Process Philosophy, Ecology, Decolonial Thought and Movement Studies. With a background in Economic Theory, Critical Theory and Philsophy, I am currently working on a project that attempts to articulate a process philosophic approaches to ecological violence.

In the future, I hope to start my own transdisciplinary research space that instigates critical interventions and resistance in the face of ecological violence. This portfolio is an attempt to articulate my iterative becomings.
